ROWS: events

COLUMNS:
-id
-e_code (event code)
-title (null possible)
-type (class, homework, deadline, meeting, study)
-start
-end (null possible)
-class (6 by tag + coaching + orientation) - foreign key
-assoc_notes (by tag)
-assoc_events (by id)
-status (upcoming, past, changed, cancelled)
-details (null possible)
-mod_start
-mod_end

COLUMNS:
-id
-start (timestamp set to 8am)
-end (timestamp set to 8pm)
-holiday (yes/no)
-title (of holiday or break)

-medium
